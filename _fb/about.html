About Andrea and Focus Beauty

“There is no cosmetic for beauty like happiness.”
- Maria Mitchell

Female beauty is to be celebrated, and as a woman working for and with women, I am excited to be apart of a growing industry that contributes to our overall well-being.

Health, cosmetics, and skin care have always been more than an interest for me, but in the last year I have been given the opportunity to grow those interests into a career at Dulce Lash, which I am utterly passionate about and grateful for.

I grew up in the Seattle area, but moved away for some time, only to return home and settle back into my Seattle roots.

I look forward to seeing where this path takes me!
